Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Choose a story from the menu, fill in the blanks with your own words (nouns, verbs, adjectives, numbers, and so on), then tap GENERATE to see your finished story.

Do I need an account?

You can start playing right away. Creating a free account lets you save your stories, appear on the leaderboard, and earn achievements.

I'm having trouble logging in or using the app.

Email us at novalibapp@gmail.com with a short description of what's happening and your device type, and we'll help you sort it out.

How do I delete my account or data?

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app, or email us at novalibapp@gmail.com and we'll take care of it for you.

How is my information handled?

We never sell or share your data. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy.
